FAQ

Q1: Does the app work offline?
Area conversion works offline. Currency conversion may require internet access to fetch the latest exchange rates.

Q2: How accurate are the conversions?
Area conversions use standard formulas. Currency results depend on the data provider’s published rates and update frequency.

Q3: What units and currencies are supported?
The app supports common real-estate units such as Ping, m², ft², and more (depending on app version), plus popular currencies such as TWD, USD, JPY, and CNY.

Q4: How do I report a bug or request a feature?
Email us with details and screenshots. Feature requests are welcome.

Troubleshooting

  • If exchange rates do not update, check your network connection and try again later.
  • Restart the app after changing iOS language/region settings.
  • If the app crashes, reinstall and contact support with your device details.
