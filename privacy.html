English

EstateConvert (“the App”) helps users convert real estate area units and currency values quickly and conveniently. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

1. Information Collection

EstateConvert does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

The App does not require account registration and does not ask for:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Precise location
  • Payment information

2. Data Processing

Area unit conversions are processed locally on your device. We do not transmit your conversion inputs or results to our servers.

3. Currency Exchange Data

For currency conversion, the App may retrieve exchange-rate data from publicly available data providers over the internet. Standard technical information (such as IP address) may be included as part of normal network communication. This information is not stored or used by the developer.

4. Advertising (If Applicable)

The App may display ads from third-party advertising providers in the future (e.g., Google AdMob). These providers may collect anonymous device information for ad delivery and measurement under their own privacy policies. If ads are enabled, we will update this policy and the App Store privacy disclosures accordingly.

5. Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services such as:

  • Exchange rate data providers
  • Advertising platforms (if enabled)
  • Analytics services (if enabled)

These services may collect limited information necessary to operate their services.

6. Children’s Privacy

EstateConvert does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.

8. Contact
